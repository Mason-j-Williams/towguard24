Responsible Disclosure Policy
TowGuard24 is committed to maintaining a secure platform for all users. We welcome and encourage security researchers, developers, and users to responsibly report vulnerabilities so we can address them quickly and effectively.
1. Purpose
This policy outlines how to report potential security vulnerabilities and how TowGuard24 will respond. We support good‑faith research and do not pursue legal action against individuals who follow this policy.

2. Reporting a Vulnerability
If you believe you’ve discovered a security issue, please report it privately.

Email:security@towguard24.com
Include the following (if possible):
Description of the vulnerability

Steps to reproduce

Potential impact

Proof‑of‑concept (optional)

Your preferred contact information
3. What You Can Expect
TowGuard24 will:

Acknowledge your report within 48 hours

Begin an initial assessment within 5 business days

Provide updates during the investigation

Work with you on coordinated disclosure

Credit your contribution if desired
4. Guidelines for Researchers
We ask that you:

Avoid accessing or modifying user data

Avoid actions that degrade service availability

Avoid privacy violations

Avoid social engineering or phishing

Avoid physical attacks on infrastructure

Give us reasonable time to remediate before public disclosure
5. Safe Harbor
TowGuard24 will not pursue legal action against researchers who:

Act in good faith

Follow this policy

Avoid harming users or systems

Do not exploit vulnerabilities beyond what is necessary for proof.
