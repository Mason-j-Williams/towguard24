Privacy Policy
TowGuard24 is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and your rights as a user of our platform.

1. Information We Collect
TowGuard24 collects only the information necessary to operate and improve our services, including:

Account information (email, username)

Device information (model, OS version)

Usage data (features used, timestamps)

Evidence data voluntarily uploaded by users

Location data when required for state‑specific legal tools

We do not collect unnecessary personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use your data to:

Provide TowGuard24 services

Improve accuracy and performance

Deliver state‑specific legal information

Generate dispute documents

Detect fraudulent towing practices

Maintain platform security

We do not sell or monetize user data.

3. Data Sharing
We may share data only with:

Service providers who support our platform

Lawful authorities when required

Users themselves (upon request)

We do not share data with advertisers or data brokers.

4. Data Security
TowGuard24 uses industry‑standard security measures including:

TLS 1.3 encryption

AES‑256 encrypted storage

Access controls and monitoring

Secure cloud infrastructure

5. Data Retention
We retain data only as long as necessary for:

Service operation

Legal compliance

User requests

Users may request deletion of their data.

6. Your Rights
You may request:

Access to your data

Correction of inaccurate data

Deletion of your data

Information about our processing

7. Updates
This policy may be updated periodically. Material changes will be communicated to users.
